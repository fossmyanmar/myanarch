Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary for the operation of our application and website:

  • Basic usage statistics (non-personal)
  • Archaeological site documentation data
  • GPS coordinates (with adjustable precision)
  • Contact information you provide voluntarily

2. How We Use Your Data

All collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Cultural heritage documentation and protection
  • Application improvement and maintenance
  • Responding to user inquiries
  • Academic research (aggregated data only)

3. Data Protection

We implement strong security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all sensitive data
  • Secure offline storage on devices
  • Regular security audits
  • No sharing with third parties without consent

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your collected data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Adjust location precision settings
